# Nesteye Legal Templates

These are **starting-point drafts** for the core legal documents Nesteye, Inc. needs as a pre-seed Edge AI company shipping hardware + software to commercial broiler farms.

## ⚠️ Important

**These templates have not been reviewed by an attorney.** Treat them as a structured starting point that captures the right shape and the Nesteye-specific facts (Texas-based, Delaware C-corp, edge-first architecture, farmer-owned imagery, integrator pathway). Before you sign anything or hand a document to a counterparty, have a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction (ideally one who does ag-tech or hardware + SaaS startups) review and customize them.

Suggested resources for finding counsel:
- Cooley GO templates (free) — https://www.cooleygo.com/
- Wilson Sonsini Term Sheet Generator (free) — https://www.wsgr.com/
- Stripe Atlas (bundles incorporation + cap table + standard founder docs)
- Clerky (more thorough than Atlas, popular with YC startups)
- Local Austin firms: many ag-tech and hardware startups use Egan Nelson, Reed Smith, or Wilson Sonsini's Austin office.

## What's in this folder

| File | What it covers | Counterparty | Priority |
|------|---------------|--------------|----------|
| `01-mutual-nda.md` | Mutual confidentiality for any preliminary conversation | Investors, integrators, farmers, contractors | Use ASAP |
| `02-pilot-program-agreement.md` | Free or low-cost pilot deployment on a farm | Pilot farmers (your 2 secured farms) | Before pilot install |
| `03-master-services-agreement.md` | Paid subscription + hardware lease/sale | Paying farmer customers | Before first paid deployment |
| `04-data-processing-agreement.md` | Addendum covering Farm Data + PII | Bundle with MSA | With MSA |
| `05-hardware-limited-warranty.md` | Warranty + RMA terms for the Edge Kit | Anyone receiving hardware | Ship with hardware |
| `06-end-user-license-agreement.md` | EULA for the mobile dashboard | All app users | Before app v1 ships |
| `07-ip-assignment-agreement.md` | Founders + contractors assign IP to the company | Fouzan, Haashir, Jawaad, contractors | Sign at incorporation |
| `08-integrator-partnership-loi.md` | LOI / term sheet for Tyson, Perdue, etc. | Integrators | Phase 2 GTM |

The Privacy Policy and Website Terms of Service are **already live on the marketing site** at `/privacy.html` and `/terms.html`. The source files are in the landing-page repo.

## Recommended sequencing

**Right now, before any conversation with money or farms attached:**
1. Sign `07-ip-assignment-agreement.md` — all three founders. Critical for fundraising diligence and to clean up any pre-incorporation IP.
2. Use `01-mutual-nda.md` for substantive talks with integrators, investors looking at sensitive technical info, and any contractor.

**Before the two secured pilot farms install:**
3. Execute `02-pilot-program-agreement.md` with each pilot farmer. This sets expectations on what you're providing, what data you can use, and what happens if it breaks.

**Before the first paying customer:**
4. Execute `03-master-services-agreement.md` (with `04-data-processing-agreement.md` attached as Exhibit B).
5. `05-hardware-limited-warranty.md` ships in the box.
6. `06-end-user-license-agreement.md` displayed on first app launch.

**Phase 2 (integrator pathway):**
7. Use `08-integrator-partnership-loi.md` as a starting term sheet with Tyson, Pilgrim's, Perdue, Sanderson, Mountaire.

## Conventions used in these templates

- `[BRACKETED_PLACEHOLDERS]` — values you need to fill in (names, dates, numbers).
- `[OPTIONAL: ...]` — sections to include or strike depending on the deal.
- `[NEGOTIATE: ...]` — known sticking points where counterparties will push back; have a plan.
