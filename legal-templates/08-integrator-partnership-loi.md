## 1. The Opportunity

Nesteye has developed an on-device edge computing system for commercial broiler barns that performs real-time piling detection, flock weight estimation, mortality detection, and environmental monitoring, with mobile alerts to farm operators. The system has been validated in field conditions with measured piling-detection average precision of 0.998 on test datasets and 0.88 on continuous field video, and has been selected for pilot deployment by **[NUMBER]** independent farms.

Integrator operates **[NUMBER]** processing facilities and has approximately **[NUMBER]** contract growers across **[STATES/REGIONS]**. The Parties wish to explore how Nesteye's system can be deployed across Integrator's grower network to reduce mortality, improve weight uniformity, and provide objective in-barn data to both growers and Integrator.

## 2. Proposed Structure

The Parties will explore the following partnership structure:

- **Integrator-subsidized hardware.** Integrator subsidizes all or part of the per-barn hardware cost (currently in the range of **$2,500–$3,500 per barn**) for participating growers.
- **Per-barn subscription.** Nesteye charges a monthly subscription (currently in the range of **$200–$400 per barn**) for the software, dashboards, alerts, analytics, model updates, and support. The Parties will negotiate whether the subscription is paid by Integrator, by the grower, or split.
- **Data sharing.** With grower consent, Integrator receives a defined set of derived data (mortality counts, weight estimates, environmental alerts) through a dedicated dashboard or API. Raw video and imagery remain on the edge device and are not transmitted off-site by default.
- **Co-funded pilot.** The Parties will jointly fund a pilot on **[NUMBER]** Integrator-affiliated farms over **[PERIOD — recommend 6 months]** to validate operational and economic outcomes before broader rollout.

## 3. Roles and Commitments (proposed)

**Nesteye will:**
- Supply Edge Kits, installation, and the monitoring service.
- Provide a dedicated integrator dashboard.
- Provide training and support to growers and Integrator field staff.
- Maintain SLAs for the service.

**Integrator will:**
- Identify and recruit pilot growers.
- Provide reasonable access to its grower network for the pilot.
- Provide weight, mortality, and condemnation data from processing facilities to allow joint validation of model outputs.
- Designate a project lead and field champion.

- **Raw barn imagery and sensor data ("Farm Data")** are owned by the grower at each Site, subject to a license to Nesteye to operate and improve the service.
