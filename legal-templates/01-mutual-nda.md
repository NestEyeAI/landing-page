## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship related to **[BRIEF_DESCRIPTION — e.g., "evaluation of Nesteye's Edge AI poultry monitoring system for deployment on Counterparty's farms" or "potential investment in Nesteye"]** (the "Purpose"). ## 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the "Discloser") to the other (the "Recipient"), in any form, that is identified as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given its nature and the circumstances of disclosure. Confidential Information includes, without limitation:

- Business plans, financial information, customer and supplier lists, pricing, and marketing strategies.
- Technical information, including machine learning models, training data, architectures, source code, firmware, hardware designs, schematics, bills of materials, manufacturing processes, and inference performance metrics.
- Product roadmaps and unreleased features.
- Information about pilots, farm partners, and integrator discussions.
- Any imagery, video, sensor data, or derived metrics from poultry barns (collectively, "Farm Data").
- The existence and content of the Parties' discussions.

Confidential Information does **not** include information that the Recipient can demonstrate: (a) was lawfully in its possession without an obligation of confidentiality before disclosure; (b) is or becomes publicly known through no fault of the Recipient; (c) is independently developed by the Recipient without use of or reference to the Discloser's Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully obtained from a third party without an obligation of confidentiality.

## 3. Obligations

The Recipient agrees to:

(a) use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
(b) protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information of similar importance, and in no event less than reasonable care;
(c) limit access to Confidential Information to its employees, contractors, advisors, and affiliates who have a need to know for the Purpose and who are bound by written confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement; and
(d) promptly notify the Discloser of any unauthorized use or disclosure of Confidential Information.

## 4. Compelled Disclosure

If the Recipient is required by law, subpoena, or court order to disclose Confidential Information, it will, to the extent legally permitted, give the Discloser prompt notice so the Discloser may seek a protective order, and will disclose only the portion of Confidential Information that is legally required.

## 5. No License; No Obligation to Proceed

Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of any Confidential Information or grants any license under any patent, copyright, trade secret, or other intellectual property right, except the limited right to use Confidential Information for the Purpose. Neither Party is obligated to enter into any further agreement or transaction. Either Party may terminate discussions at any time.

## 6. Term and Return

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **[TERM — recommend 3] years**, or until terminated by either Party on thirty (30) days' written notice. The confidentiality obligations in Section 3 survive for **[SURVIVAL — recommend 5] years** after the Effective Date, except that obligations regarding trade secrets continue for as long as the information remains a trade secret under applicable law.

Upon written request, the Recipient will return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession and certify destruction in writing, except that the Recipient may retain one archival copy in secure storage solely to verify compliance with this Agreement and as required by law or its bona fide records retention policies.

## 7. No Warranty

Confidential Information is provided "AS IS." Neither Party makes any representation or warranty as to the accuracy, completeness, or non-infringement of any Confidential Information disclosed.
