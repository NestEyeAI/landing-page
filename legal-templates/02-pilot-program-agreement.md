## Recitals

A. Nesteye has developed an Edge AI monitoring system for commercial broiler chicken barns consisting of overhead infrared cameras, an on-device compute unit, environmental sensors, a mobile dashboard, and machine learning software that detects piling events, estimates flock weight, monitors environmental conditions, and delivers real-time alerts (collectively, the "System").

B. Farmer operates **[NUMBER]** broiler barn(s) at the location(s) described in **Exhibit A** (the "Pilot Site") and wishes to participate in a pilot deployment of the System.

C. The Parties wish to set out the terms on which Nesteye will install, operate, and evaluate the System at the Pilot Site.

## 1. The Pilot

### 1.1 Scope
Nesteye will, at no charge to Farmer (or for the pilot fee set out in Exhibit A, if any), install the System in the barn(s) identified in **Exhibit A**, operate the System during the Pilot Term, and provide Farmer with access to the Nesteye mobile dashboard.

### 1.2 What's Included
- **Hardware:** As described in **Exhibit A** (the "Pilot Hardware"). Typical Pilot Hardware includes 6 to 8 PoE infrared cameras, one NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano compute unit, a PoE switch, an outdoor enclosure, cabling, and an environmental sensor array.
- **Software:** Edge AI inference models, dashboard access, alert delivery, and over-the-air model updates.
- **Installation:** Site survey, mounting, cabling, network setup, and commissioning by Nesteye personnel or a Nesteye-supervised installer.
- **Support:** Email and phone support during business hours. Best-effort response to critical issues within 24 hours.

### 1.3 What's Not Included
- Structural modifications to the barn beyond mounting brackets and cable runs.
- Provision of internet connectivity (Farmer is responsible for providing a working internet connection or cellular hotspot at the Pilot Site).
- Replacement of unrelated farm infrastructure.

## 2. Pilot Term

### 2.1 Term
The pilot begins on the Installation Date and continues for **[PILOT_DURATION — recommend 90 to 180 days]** (the "Pilot Term"), unless extended by mutual written agreement or terminated earlier as provided in Section 9.

### 2.2 Success Criteria
The Parties will jointly evaluate the System against the success criteria listed in **Exhibit B**. Either Party may propose to convert the pilot to a paid engagement under a Master Services Agreement at any time during or following the Pilot Term.

## 3. Hardware Ownership and Access

### 3.1 Ownership
The Pilot Hardware remains the property of Nesteye throughout the Pilot Term. Title does not pass to Farmer.

### 3.2 Risk of Loss
Nesteye bears the risk of loss for the Pilot Hardware, except for damage caused by Farmer's willful misconduct or gross negligence.

### 3.3 Site Access
Farmer grants Nesteye and its authorized personnel reasonable access to the Pilot Site during normal business hours, on reasonable prior notice, for installation, maintenance, inspection, removal, and to conduct technical or model evaluation. Nesteye will comply with Farmer's biosecurity protocols and applicable safety rules.

### 3.4 Removal
At the end of the Pilot Term (if not converted to a paid engagement), Nesteye will, at its expense, remove the Pilot Hardware within **[REMOVAL_PERIOD — recommend 30] days** and restore the Pilot Site to substantially its prior condition, reasonable wear and tear excepted.

## 4. Data

### 4.1 Farm Data
"Farm Data" means the raw video, imagery, sensor readings, and environmental data captured by the System at the Pilot Site. **As between the Parties, Farmer owns the Farm Data.**

### 4.2 License to Nesteye
Farmer grants Nesteye a worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive license to access, store, copy, process, transmit, analyze, and use Farm Data to (a) operate the System for Farmer; (b) provide alerts and analytics; (c) train, validate, fine-tune, and improve Nesteye's machine learning models; (d) develop new features and products; and (e) create de-identified and aggregated datasets that do not identify Farmer or the Pilot Site.

### 4.3 Derived Data
"Derived Data" means metadata, inferences, metrics, model outputs, statistics, and other information generated by the System from Farm Data. **Nesteye owns all Derived Data**, subject to Farmer's license to use Derived Data for its own farm operations.

### 4.4 Edge-First Architecture
The Parties acknowledge that the System is designed so that real-time video remains on the Edge AI device at the Pilot Site, and only Derived Data, operational telemetry, and alerts are transmitted off-site by default. Nesteye may request Farmer's consent to upload limited Farm Data samples for model improvement; Farmer's consent will not be unreasonably withheld.

### 4.5 Confidentiality
Nesteye will treat the Pilot Site location, Farmer's operational practices, and any Farmer business information as Confidential Information under the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement between the Parties dated **[NDA_DATE]**, which is incorporated by reference. If no NDA is in place, the confidentiality terms in **Exhibit C** apply.

## 5. Farmer Cooperation

Farmer agrees to:
- Provide a representative as the primary point of contact for the pilot.
- Provide reasonable feedback on System performance during the Pilot Term.
- Notify Nesteye promptly of any System malfunction, alert, or unexpected behavior.
- Maintain biosecurity, electrical safety, and physical integrity of the Pilot Site.
- Not move, modify, or attempt to reverse engineer the Pilot Hardware or software.

## 6. Intellectual Property

Nesteye retains all right, title, and interest in and to the System, including all hardware designs, firmware, software, models, algorithms, documentation, and any improvements developed during the pilot. No license is granted to Farmer except the limited right to use the System for the duration of the Pilot Term to monitor the Pilot Site.

## 7. Animal Welfare and Intervention

The V1 System detects events and provides alerts. It does not autonomously intervene with the flock. If a future version of the System includes intervention features (e.g., LED dispersal, audio dispersal), those features will be enabled only with Farmer's express written consent and subject to additional terms in **Exhibit D**.
