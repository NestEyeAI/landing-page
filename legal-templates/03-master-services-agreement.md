# NESTEYE MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

**This Master Services Agreement** (this "Agreement") is entered into as of **[EFFECTIVE_DATE]** (the "Effective Date") by and between:

- **Nesteye, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business in Austin, Texas ("Nesteye"); and
- **[CUSTOMER_NAME]**, a **[ENTITY_TYPE]** with a principal place of business at **[CUSTOMER_ADDRESS]** ("Customer").

## 1. Definitions

- **"Order Form"** means an ordering document signed by both Parties referencing this Agreement and specifying the Edge Kits, Sites, fees, and term.
- **"Edge Kit"** means the hardware bundle Nesteye provides per barn, described in the applicable Order Form, including cameras, compute unit, switch, enclosure, cabling, and environmental sensors.
- **"Services"** means the Nesteye Edge AI monitoring service, including operation of the Edge Kit, machine learning inference, mobile dashboard access, alert delivery, over-the-air model updates, and customer support, as described on the applicable Order Form.
- **"Site"** means a barn location identified in an Order Form where an Edge Kit is deployed.
- **"Farm Data"** means raw video, imagery, sensor readings, and environmental data captured by the Edge Kit at a Site.
- **"Derived Data"** means metadata, inferences, metrics, model outputs, and other information generated by the Services from Farm Data.
- **"Subscription Term"** means the duration of Customer's right to receive the Services for a given Site as set forth in the applicable Order Form.

## 2. Provision of the Services

### 2.1 Services
Nesteye will use commercially reasonable efforts to provide the Services in accordance with the Order Form and the Service Level commitments in **Exhibit A**.

### 2.2 Edge Kits
Customer's rights to Edge Kits are set out in the Order Form and depend on whether the Order Form designates the Edge Kit as **purchased** by Customer or **leased** from Nesteye:

- **Purchased Edge Kits.** Title passes to Customer on delivery and full payment. Nesteye's hardware warranty (separate document) governs hardware defects.
- **Leased Edge Kits.** Title remains with Nesteye. Customer has a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to use the Edge Kit at the Site during the Subscription Term.

### 2.3 Installation
Installation is provided by Nesteye or a Nesteye-authorized installer for the fee on the Order Form. Customer will provide reasonable site access, electrical power, internet connectivity (or cellular signal sufficient for a hotspot), and biosecurity briefing.

### 2.4 Updates
Nesteye may push over-the-air firmware and model updates at any time. Updates are designed to be backward-compatible and to auto-roll-back on failure. Customer authorizes Nesteye to deploy updates remotely without prior approval for non-material changes; Nesteye will provide reasonable advance notice for changes that materially alter Service behavior.

### 2.5 Support
Nesteye will provide the support described in **Exhibit B**. Support hours are **[HOURS]** business days. Critical issues that materially impair the Services will be addressed on a 24/7 best-effort basis.

## 3. Customer Responsibilities

Customer will:
- Use the Services only at the Sites listed on Order Forms and only for monitoring its own poultry operations.
- Comply with applicable laws, integrator contracts, animal welfare requirements, and the acceptable use rules in **Exhibit C**.
- Maintain the physical environment at each Site (electrical, biosecurity, structural).
- Keep Edge Kit credentials confidential and not attempt to modify, reverse engineer, copy, or tamper with the Edge Kit or Services.
- Notify Nesteye of any malfunction, suspected security incident, or unauthorized access.

## 5. Data

### 5.1 Farm Data Ownership
**Customer owns the Farm Data.** Customer grants Nesteye a worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive license to access, store, process, transmit, analyze, and use Farm Data to (a) provide and improve the Services for Customer; (b) train, validate, fine-tune, and improve Nesteye's machine learning models; (c) develop new features and products; and (d) create de-identified and aggregated datasets that do not identify Customer or any Site.

### 5.2 Derived Data Ownership
**Nesteye owns Derived Data.** Nesteye grants Customer a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use Derived Data relating to Customer's own Sites for Customer's internal farm operations and reporting.

### 5.3 Edge-First Architecture
Real-time video remains on the Edge Kit by default. Only Derived Data, operational telemetry, and alerts are transmitted off-site by default. Nesteye may upload limited Farm Data samples for model improvement consistent with Customer's preferences as configured in the dashboard.

### 5.4 Data Processing Addendum
The Parties' obligations regarding the handling of personal information are set out in the Data Processing Addendum attached as **Exhibit D**, which is incorporated by reference.

### 5.5 Integrator Data Sharing
If Customer is a contract grower for a poultry integrator (e.g., Tyson, Pilgrim's Pride, Perdue, Sanderson, Mountaire), Customer may direct Nesteye to share specific Derived Data with the integrator. Customer represents that it has the authority to make such direction.
